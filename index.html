Getting to The Roost from
Hackney Town Hall

The easiest way to get to London is by train and use public transport to get around, parking is very limited and can be very expensive. The nearest overground station is Hackney Central which is around a 3-5 mins walk. If you are driving, there is pay and display parking all around the Town Hall.

The Roost is 0.7 miles from Hackney Town Hall and according to google a 15 min walk. If you prefer to get a cab, you can use either Hailo (you can book in advance from their website) or Uber to get a black cab or Addison Lee to book a mini cab. The fare should be between £5 and £10.
